Deprecated "Monte Carlo" aliases

The following are deprecated and are just aliases to the Ensemble equivalents:

  • AbstractMonteCarloProblem: AbstractEnsembleProblem
  • MonteCarloProblem: EnsembleProblem
  • MonteCarloAlgorithm: EnsembleAlgorithm
  • AbstractMonteCarloSolution: AbstractEnsembleSolution
  • MonteCarloSolution: EnsembleSolution
  • MonteCarloSummary: EnsembleSummary
  • calculate_monte_errors: calculate_ensemble_errors
